Unlimited Mailing Lists

All domains can have an unlimited number of email lists. Using our control panel, you can setup and configure detailed mailing list settings.

Through the mailing list control panel you can configure viewing and posting rights for your list, store list archives, manage list administrators, moderate posts to the lists, invite new members, modify sending preferences, configure list settings and much more.

Our mailing lists are powered by Mailman, an open source mailing list engine. For more information on Mailman, visit the program's official website.
