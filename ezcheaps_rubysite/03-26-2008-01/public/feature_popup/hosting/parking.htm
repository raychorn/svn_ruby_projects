Parked Domain Names

A parked domain is an additional domain name that points to your main website. Parked domain names are useful if you use multiple domain names to access your website.

Unlike domain pointers, parked domain names can not point to their own website and can not be used for email. They simply point to your main website.

You may add as many parked domains as you like with any of our hosting plans.
