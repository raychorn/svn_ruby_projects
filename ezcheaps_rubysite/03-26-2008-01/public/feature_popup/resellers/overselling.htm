Disk Space & Bandwidth Overselling

Although the term "overselling" generally has negative connotations, in the context of a reseller plan, overselling is a huge benefit. The ability to "oversell" allows you to maximize use of your disk space and bandwidth, rather than letting it go to waste.

Each reseller plan provides you with a pool of disk space and a pool of bandwidth that is shared between your resold accounts. When you create a resold hosting account in your WHM control panel, you set quotas for disk space and bandwidth (either explicitly or by assigning it to one of your hosting packages).

The term "overselling" means that we only count the space and bandwidth that are actually used against your total pool. So even if your disk space pool is 10 GB, you could create 50 x 1 GB accounts and be fine as long as the amount of disk space actually used by those accounts is less than 10 GB (the size of your pool).
