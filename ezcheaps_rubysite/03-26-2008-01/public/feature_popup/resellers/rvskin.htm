RVSkin Control Panel Interface

Designed with resellers in mind, RVSkin is a skin package that adds usability, functionality and additional branding capabilities to the standard cPanel/WHM control panel. It is hands down the most complete and thoroughly crafted skin available for cPanel/WHM.

RVSkin allows for complete reseller functionality and control, giving you the ability to brand your customers' control panels, add/remove access to specific features, and login to their account as an administrator. Additionally, RVSkin is currently available in 18 languages and a host of colors/styles.

For more information, a demo and an in-depth explanation of the functionality added by RVSkin, visit the official website.
