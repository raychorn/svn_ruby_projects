What is a blog?

A "blog," shortened from weblog, is a popular new publishing medium on the web. Originally intended for online journal uses, blogs have spread into many areas and into popular culture. Creating and maintaining a blog is simple: first, one of our scripts instantly installs the software for you, then you just follow the instructions of your blog of choice to start posting. Blogs are a great way to post content and connect with your visitors and take very little web experience to run.
