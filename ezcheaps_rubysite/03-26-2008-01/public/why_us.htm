Healthy Hosting & Overselling

Q: You promise a great deal of features for a rather low price. Aren't you guilty of "overselling" your services?

A: "Overselling" is a fairly hot-button term in the web hosting industry right now. Although we do promise a large amount of features for a low price, we are trying to offer a great deal and remain competitive. Our Healthy Hosting philosophy still extends to "overselling" - we never promise more resources than we are capable of delivering.

While we understand that not every client may need to use 100% of their resources, we provide ample room for each client to use their share. Our top-of-the-line server hardware combined with our automated monitoring and response system allow us to offer reliable service and guaranteed performance.

In addition, we have found that while space and bandwidth can be promised and delivered in relatively large numbers, many hosts end up limiting users by their CPU and resource usage. We are one of the few hosts that have set limits on sites-per-server ratios, so depending on your plan, you are guaranteed a certain up-front share of CPU power, RAM, etc. as well.
