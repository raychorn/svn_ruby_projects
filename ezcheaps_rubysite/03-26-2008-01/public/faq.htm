    My website gets a lot of traffic--will this be a problem?

    This usually depends on the nature of your website. While relatively straightforward sites (think basic html/xhtml content, most shopping sites, webpages, blogs, etc.) can receive tens of thousands of hits at any given time and work perfectly fine, problems can arise with more complex sites--sites with lots of images, scripts or interactivity with visitors. Tens of thousands of users each accessing a database at the same time can cause quite a strain on a server and might be too much for a shared web hosting environment.

    The thing to remember is that the term "shared hosting" (which is another term for standard web hosting) means that your site will share a server with other users. This allows for plenty of usage while keeping prices low, but certain sites may not be appropriate for such an environment. If you are unsure whether your high volume website will work with our shared plans, please feel free to e-mail our sales department, and we will be happy to assist you in determining the best solution for your needs.

    What do you mean by "low site to server ratios" and how does this benefit me?

    The number of accounts hosted on a web server can have a significant impact on how quickly that server is able to respond to page requests. Since all users of a server share system resources such as processor time and RAM, a server with too many accounts can be slow to respond and cause your site's visitors to become frustrated and leave.

    Many hosting companies pack too many accounts onto each server in an effort to maximize profit. At EzCheapSites.Com, we feel that the long term customer satisfaction resulting from providing high performance hosting is far more important than the short term profits made by overfilling servers. Our customers love the extra speed they get at EzCheapSites.Com, making them more than happy to recommend EzCheapSites.Com to their friends, family and website visitors.

    Join EzCheapSites.Com today and experience the many benefits of our "undercrowded" servers!

    What is "available server power" and how does it benefit me?

    The amount of server power or server system resources available to each hosting account depends on the number of other websites sharing a given web server. A server's total processor and memory (RAM) is shared by all of the accounts on that server.

    All of our accounts are hosted on servers with much fewer accounts than the majority of other web hosts. But, above and beyond that, accounts on our Gold plan will be on servers with half as many accounts as servers hosting Silver plan accounts.

    Servers hosting Diamond plan customers will have one quarter as many accounts as the Silver plan. This ultra low site to server ratio means that each individual Diamond hosting account has roughly four times the share of the server's processor and memory as does a Silver account. That's a lot of power!

    What is the difference between NetAdmin and Backstage? Which one should I use to manage my account?

    Backstage is our central customer control panel. It allows you to view your billing and contact information, manage all of your EzCheapSites.Com services, view and submit new support tickets, buy, transfer and manage domain names, view important announcements from EzCheapSites.Com customer service and grant other users access to your account.

    NetAdmin is the control panel used to manage features of a specific web hosting account. It allows you to manage email features and mySQL databases, autoinstall scripts, view web statistics and much, much more. Through Backstage you can access the NetAdmin control panels for all of your hosting accounts without needing to login to a separate interface for each one.

    You can access every part of your account from within Backstage. Backstage provides automatic login to NetAdmin, so there is no need to login to NetAdmin directly. To login to Backstage, simply point your browser to https://backstage.EzCheapSites.Com/ or click the "Customer Login" link at the very top of the main EzCheapSites.Com website.

    What is your Resource Usage Policy? If my site gets busy, will it be suspended?

    In shared web hosting, the ability to use your account is limited not just by your space and bandwidth, but also by how much of your server's resources (processor and RAM) you may be able to use at a given time. Since you are sharing a server with many other clients, each must operate their site within certain limits to ensure that all accounts perform as well as expected.

    We also completely understand, however, that webmasters are often unaware of instances where their site is using too many resources, and that such usage is not done maliciously. Our Level 3 staff is partially dedicated to handling resource usage issues and working with clients to resolve them.
